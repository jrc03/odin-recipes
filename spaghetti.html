Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It is simple yet delicious, typically served with spaghetti noodles.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta or guanciale (diced)
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Pecorino Romano cheese (grated)
  • Black pepper (freshly ground)
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain.
  2. In a large skillet, cook pancetta or guanciale over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan cheese, Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Add cooked pasta to the skillet with pancetta and toss to coat in the rendered fat.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, adding reserved pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Season with salt and more black pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
